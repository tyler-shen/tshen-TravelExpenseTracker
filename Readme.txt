appName: tshen_TravelExpenseTracker.apk

Describe: This app is used for tracking your expense in travel. You can simplely click "add a claim" to record your trip. Then click the trip showed in the list to add expense items to it, edit it, approve it, email it and delete it. Once you click "Add an expense item", it will show you a layout to record your expense, and then you can click the expense item showed in the list to edit it and delete it.
